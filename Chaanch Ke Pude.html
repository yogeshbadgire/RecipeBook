Chaanch Ke Pude



Ingredients

  • Dahi - grams
  • Water - grams
  • Wheat Flour - grams
  • Garlic - grams (make a paste)
  • Homemade Thecha - TSP
  • Salt - To Taste
  • Lemon Juice - - drops

Instructions

  1. Make the buttermilk.
  2. In a bowl, take flour, buttermilk, garlic paste, and homemade thecha and mix with a whisk until a smooth batter is formed.
  3. Make the pude on a pan.

Notes:

  • Make the buttermilk in batches if your mixer grinder jar is not big enough.
