Paneer Lababdar



Ingredients

Gravy
  • Oil - TBSP
  • Jeera - TSP
  • Garlic - cloves
  • Ginger - inch
  • Green Chillies - Nos.
  • Onions - (medium-sized) sliced
  • Tej Patta - Nos.
  • Cinnamon - inch
  • Green Cardamom - Nos.
  • Tomatoes - (medium-sized) chopped
  • Coriander Stems - TBSP
  • Salt - To Taste
  • Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder - TSP
  • Turmeric Powder - TSP
  • Cashews -
  • Magaz Seeds - TBSP
Paneer Coating
  • Oil - TBSP
  • Paneer - grams
  • Turmeric Powder - TSP
  • Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder - TSP
  • Salt - A pinch
Final Sabji
  • Oil - TBSP
  • Cumin Seeds - TSP
  • Green Chillies - Nos.
  • Onions - (medium-sized) chopped
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - TBSP
  • Turmeric Powder - TSP
  • Coriander Powder - TSP
  • Tomatoes - (medium-sized) chopped
  • Salt - To Taste
  • Capsicum - Nos. (chopped)
  • Fresh Cream - TBSP
  • Butter - TBSP
  • Kasuri Methi - TSP
  • Garam Masala - A pinch
  • Cheese Cube - Nos. (grated)
  • Fresh Coriander - A handful

Instructions

Gravy
  1. Take a pan and heat oil.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let crackle.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, green chillies, onions, tej patta, cinnamon, and green cardamom and cook on high flame until onions become translucent.
  4. Once the onions are translucent, add tomatoes, coriander stems, salt, kashmiri red chilli powder, turmeric powder, cashews, and magaz seeds and cook on medium flame until tomatoes are mushy.
  5. Add hot water and cover the pan and cook for atleast 10-12 minutes.
  6. Remove tej patta and cinnamon and let the mixture cool down.
  7. Grind the mixture and make a smooth and fine paste.
  8. Strain the gravy and keep it aside for further use.
Paneer Coating
  1. Take a pan and heat oil in it.
  2. Add paneer cubes, turmeric powder, kashmiri red chilli powder, and salt and toss for a bit until the panner cubes are coated well.
  3. Keep it aside for further use.
Final Sabji
  1. Take a pan and heat oil in it.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let crackle.
  3. Add chopped green chillies, chopped onions and saute on high flame until the onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  5. Lower the flame and add turmeric powder, kashmiri red chilli powder, coriander powder, and hot water and cook well.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and salt cook until tomatoes are completely mushy.
  7. Add chopped capsicum and cook for a minute.
  8. Add gravy and cook for 5-6 minutes and add a pinch of sugar.
  9. Add paneer, fresh cream, butter, kasuri methi, garam masala, and cheese.
  10. Add fresh coriander.
  11. Paneer Lababdar is ready.
