Caramalized Onions and Garlic Spaghetti



Ingredients

  • Onion - Large (thinly sliced)
  • Garlic - Cloves (minced)
  • Chilli Crisp - TBSP
  • Fresh Cream (250ml Pack) -
  • Soy Sauce - TBSP
  • Cheese - Cup (grated)
  • Spaghetti Pasta - grams (dry weight)
  • Butter - TBSP
  • Olive Oil - TBSP
  • Parsley - For garnishing
  • Seasonings Mix
    • Red Chilli Powder - TSP
    • Garlic Powder - TSP
    • Salt - TSP
    • Black Pepper - TSP
    • Italian Seasoning - TSP

Instructions

  1. Take a pan and heat butter and oil together.
  2. Add the sliced onions and cook on low heat until they are caramelized (about 20-30 minutes).
  3. While the onions are cooking, boil water in a separate pot and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  4. Drain the pasta but DON'T throw away the pasta water
  5. Once the onions are caramelized, add garlic along with the Seasonings Mix.
  6. Cook for a minute and add chilli crisp and saute for a minute.
  7. Pour the fresh cream and let it simmer for a few minutes and then add grated cheese.
  8. Mix well until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.
  9. If the sauce becomes too thick, add some pasta water to make it silky.
  10. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss it well to coat the pasta with the sauce.
  11. Serve hot, garnished with extra cheese if you want and a sprinkle of chopped parsley.
