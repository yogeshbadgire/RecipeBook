Masale Bhaat Recipe



Ingredients

Green Vatan

  • Fresh Coconut - grams
  • Coriander - A handful
  • Ginger - TBSP
  • Green Chillies - Nos.

Masale Bhaat

  • Rice - grams
  • Oil - TBSP
  • Mustard Seeds - TBSP
  • Masala Mix 1
    • Cumin Seeds - TBSP
    • Curry Leaves - Nos.
    • Bay Leaves - Nos.
    • Black Pepper -
    • Star Anise - Nos.
    • Cloves - Nos.
    • Cinnamon - inch
  • Cashews - TBSP
  • Vegetables
    • Brinjal -
    • Tondali - Nos.
    • Potatoes - Nos.
    • Peas - cup
  • Ghee - TBSP
  • Coriander - A handful
  • Masala Mix 2
    • Goda Masala - TSP
    • Garam Masala - TBSP
    • Coriander Powder - TBSP
    • Turmeric Powder - TSP
  • Water - grams

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice well and keep aside.
  2. Make the Green Vatan and keep aside.
  3. Take a pressure cooker and heat oil.
  4. Add mustard seeds and let crackle.
  5. Add Masala Mix 1 and cashews and cook for 30 seconds.
  6. Add Vegetables and salt and cover and cook until Vegetables soften.
  7. Add Masala Mix 2 and cook until oil separates.
  8. Add washed rice, green vatan, salt, hot water, coriander and ghee and mix well.
  9. Bring it to boil.
  10. Now close the lid of the cooker and cook for 3 whistles.
  11. Let the pressure cooker depressurize naturally.
  12. Masala Bhaat is ready.
