Soya Curry Recipe (Pressure Cook Method)



Ingredients

  • Boiled Soya Wadi - grams (dry)
  • Curd - g
  • Cashews - pieces
  • Ginger - inch
  • Green Chillies - pieces
  • Masala Mix 1
    • Turmeric Powder - TSP
    • Coriander Powder - TBSP
    • Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder - TBSP
    • Red Chilli Powder - TSP
    • Cumin Powder - TSP
    • Garam Masala Powder - TSP
  • Mustard Oil - TBSP
  • Masala Mix 2
    • Cumin Seeds - TSP
    • Cinnamon - inch
    • Green Cardamom - pieces
    • Bay Leaf - pieces
    • Asafoetida - TSP
  • Salt - To taste
  • Hot water - As required
  • Roasted Kasuri Methi - To taste
  • Garam Masala Powder - TSP
  • Green Chillies - sliced
  • Fresh Coriander - As per preference

Instructions

  1. Take a mixer jar and add dahi, cashews, ginger, green chillies, and masala mixture 1 and blend.
  2. To boil the soya chunks, boil water in a pot, add salt and add the soya chunks only after the water is roaring boiling and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. After boiling, drain the soya chunks and rinse them under cold water. Squeeze out excess water and set aside.
  4. In a pressure cooker, heat mustard oil and add masala mixture 2. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add the blended mixture from the mixer jar and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the boiled soya chunks and mix well and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Add hot water as required to adjust the consistency. Close the lid and cook for 2 whistles.
  8. After the pressure is released, open the lid and add roasted kasuri methi, garam masala powder, sliced green chillies, and fresh coriander. Mix well.
  9. Soya curry is ready.
