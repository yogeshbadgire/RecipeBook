Corn Rice and Raita



Corn Rice

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice - grams
  • Water - grams
  • Corn - cup (boiled)
  • Cumin - TSP
  • Cloves -
  • Cinnamon - inch
  • Onion - medium-sized & sliced
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - TSP
  • Masala Mix 1
    • Red Chilli Powder - TSP
    • Turmeric Powder - TSP
    • Coriander Powder - TSP
    • Garam Masala Powder - TSP
  • Mint & Coriander - TBSP (chopped)
  • Kasuri Methi - TBSP
  • Salt - To taste
  • Lemon Juice - drops
  • Ghee - TBSP

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice 2-3 times or until the water runs clear.
  2. Soak the rice for at least 15-20 minutes.
  3. Take a pan and heat ghee.
  4. Also on separate stove, start boiling water on low heat to be used in the later stage.
  5. Add cumin seeds and let it crackle.
  6. Add cloves and cinnamon and roast for a little bit.
  7. Add the chopped onions and cook until the onions turn golden in color.
  8. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for a few seconds.
  9. Now reduce the heat completely and add Masala Mix 1.
  10. Add chopped mint, coriander leaves, and kasuri methi and fry for a few seconds.
  11. Add the boiled corn and mix well.
  12. Drain the rice completely and add it to the corn-masala mixture and fry for half a minute.
  13. Add the boiled water and adjust the salt to taste.
  14. Sprinkle some drops of lemon juice.
  15. Bring the mixture to boil and then reduce the heat to a sim.
  16. Cover the pan and allow the rice to cook for 5 minutes (keep a timer).
  17. After 5 minutes, turn off the heat and allow the rice to cook further for another 15 minutes keeping it undisturbed. Don't open the lid.
  18. After 15 minutes are up, open the pan and gently fluff up the rice using a fork and cover the pan again for 2 minutes or until you are ready to eat.
  19. Serve the rice with raita.

Raita

Ingredients

  • Dahi - grams
  • Water - grams
  • Onion - medium-sized (chopped)
  • Tomato - medium-sized (chopped)
  • Black Pepper Powder - A pinch

Instructions

  1. Take bowl and add dahi.
  2. Whisk the dahi until it becomes completely smooth.
  3. Add water, onions, tomatoes, salt, and black pepper powder and mix well.
  4. Serve with the rice.
