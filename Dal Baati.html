Dal Baati (Fried)



Ingredients

Baati
  • Baati Flour - grams
  • Oil - grams
  • Ajwain - TSP
  • Salt - TSP
  • Baking Soda - TSP
  • Water - grams
Dal
  • Yet to be measured because I don't make it. 😒

Instructions

Baati
  1. Mix all the ingredients and make a dough.
  2. Keep it aside and covered for half an hour.
  3. Make small balls of the dough each weighing around 120 grams.
  4. Take each ball and separate a small amount of the dough.
  5. Make a small ball of the separated portion and oil it a little bit.
  6. Now place the small ball inside the remaining portion of the dough and seal it.
  7. Shape them like a solid donut and steam them for 25 minutes.
  8. Let them cool and then cut each ball into 4 small pieces.
  9. Fry them in batches until brown.
