Veg Dum Biryani



Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice - grams
  • Water - As required
  • Onions - grams
  • Garlic - cloves
  • Ginger - inch
  • Green Chillies - Nos.
  • Tomato - medium-sized
  • Coriander - A handful
  • Mint - A handful
  • Carrots - medium-sized (large chopped)
  • Beans - pieces
  • Cauliflower - 1-inch pieces
  • Potato - medium-sized (large chopped)
  • Green Peas - TBSP
  • Oil - TBSP
  • Masala Mix 1
    • Turmeric Powder - TSP
    • Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder - TSP
    • Red Chilli Powder - TSP
    • Coriander Powder - TSP
    • Biryani Masala - TSP
  • Dahi - grams (whipped)
  • Masala Mix 2
    • Bay Leaf - Nos.
    • Cinnamon - inch
    • Cloves - pieces
    • Star Anise - pieces
    • Black Peppercorns - pieces
    • Shah Jeera - TSP
  • Lemon Juice - pieces
  • Green Chilli - pieces

Instructions

  1. Take a flat bottom kadai and pour the oil.
  2. Add chopped onions and let it cook until they are brown. It will take around 20 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, move on to the next steps while keeping an eye on the onions.
  4. Wash the rice atleast 3-4 times or until the water runs clear.
  5. Soak the rice in clean water for atleast an hour (and maximum of 90 minutes).
  6. Prepare all other vegetables and masala mixes while the onions are cooking.
  7. Once the onions are cooked completely, add ginger, garlic, and green chillies mixture and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  8. Now add the Masala Mix 1 along with a little hot water and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Add the whipped Dahi along with salt and tomatoes and cook for 10 more minutes on low flame.
  10. Make the gravy a little flowy as the rice later will absorb much of the moisture.
  11. Meanwhile, take a pot and pour enough water to cook the rice.
  12. Add the Masala Mix 2, lemon juice along with lemon, and green chilli and turn on the stove.
  13. Bring the water to boil and wait for 2-3 minutes for the water to absorb all the flavors from the masalas.
  14. Now take the masalas out of the water with a strainer and add the saoked rice carefully to not break the grains.
  15. Cook the rice until it is 70% cooked which will take around 8-9 minutes.
  16. Once the rice is cooked, strain the rice and add it to the gravy which was cooking on the side.
  17. Do not mix the rice and gravy. The gravy should be on the bottm layer and the rice should be on the top layer.
  18. Garnish with coriander and mint and cover the pot with a clothed lid and place any heavy item on it to seal it.
  19. Now take a tawa and place it on the stove.
  20. Place the prepared biryani kadai on the tawa and keep the flame on high for 2-3 minutes.
  21. After 2-3 minutes, turn the flame to low and keep cooking for another 20 minutes.
  22. After 20 minutes, the biryani is ready to serve.

Notes:

  • Chop the onions beforehand to start the cooking as it will take more time.
  • You can also add paneer cubes along with dahi and tomatoes.
