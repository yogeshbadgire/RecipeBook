Chhole for Chaat



Ingredients

  • Kabuli Chana - 125 grams
  • Black Cardamom - 1
  • Bay Leaves - 2
  • Cloves - 3
  • Baking Soda - 1 Large pinch
  • Coriander - Preference
  • Salt - To taste
  • Ginger Chilli Paste -
  • Green Chillies - 2
  • Ginger - 1 inch
  • Salt - To taste
  • Ghee - 1 TBSP
  • Cumin Seeds - A large pinch
  • Hing - 1/4 TSP
  • Masala Mix 1 -
    • Black Salt - 1 TSP
    • Red Chilli Powder - 1 TSP
    • Yellow Chilli Powder - 1 TSP
    • Coriander Powder - 0.5 TBSP
    • Garam Masala - 1 TSP
  • Tamarind Water - 1 lemon sized ball
  • Ginger - 1 inch
  • Kasuri Methi - 1 pinch (roasted and crushed)
  • Coriander - As per preference

Instructions

  1. Soak chana for 7-8 hours OR overnight.
  2. Discard the water.
  3. Take mortar and pestle and add green chillies, ginger, and salt and make paste.
  4. Take pressure cooker and add soaked chana, black cardamom, bay leaves, cloves, baking soda, salt.
  5. Add water 1 inch above the chana.
  6. Turn on the stove on high flame for first whistle and then keep on medium flame for next 4-5 whistles.
  7. Turn off the gas and let the cooker depressurize naturally.
  8. Take out the cardamom and bay leaves and discard.
  9. Take kadai and add ghee.
  10. Add cumin seeds and let crackle.
  11. Add hing and ginger chilli paste and cook for a minute.
  12. Add cooked chhole, masala mix 1 , tamarind water and stir well.
  13. Cook for 7-8 minutes
  14. Mash some chhole to make thick consistency.
  15. Add ginger, kasuri methi, and coriander.
  16. Chhole are ready.
