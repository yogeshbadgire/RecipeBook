Masala Khichdi Recipe



Ingredients

  • Rice - grams
  • Moong Dal - grams
  • Oil - TSP
  • Mustard Seeds - TSP
  • Curry Leaves -
  • Potato - small (cubed)
  • Onion - medium (chopped)
  • Garlic - cloves (grated)
  • Tomato - medium (finely chopped)
  • Peanuts - grams
  • Masala Mixture
    • Turmeric Powder - TSP
    • Red Chilli Powder - TSP
    • Kala Masala - TSP
  • Salt - To Taste
  • Water - grams

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and moong dal together for atleast 2-3 times.
  2. Keep the washed rice and moong dal aside and let it soak for half an hour.
  3. Start heating water in a separate pot.
  4. Take a pressure cooker and heat oil.
  5. Add mustard seeds and let crackle.
  6. Add curry leaves, garlic and onions and cook until onions are golden brown.
  7. Add potatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add tomato and sprinkle some salt and cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  9. Add peanuts and the Masala Mixture and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  10. Add the soaked rice and moong dal and mix well.
  11. Pour the hot water into the cooker and mix everything well.
  12. Close the lid of the cooker and cook for 3 whistles on medium flame and for 1 whistle on low flame.
  13. Turn off the flame and let the pressure release naturally.
  14. Open the lid and mix the khichdi well.
  15. Serve hot with papad and pickle.
  16. Enjoy your delicious Masala Khichdi!
